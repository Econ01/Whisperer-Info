# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs the manner in which Ali Cem Çakmak ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User", "you", or "your") of the Whisperer application (the "App") available on macOS and iOS devices. 

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Policy outlines our data handling practices and the choices you have regarding your information. By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our fundamental principle is data minimization and user privacy. Whisperer is engineered to operate entirely on-device (offline). Therefore, we do not collect, transmit, store, or otherwise process any of your personal data on our servers.

Specifically, we **do not** collect:
- **Audio and Media Files:** Any audio or video files you provide to the App.
- **Transcriptions:** The text output generated by the App's speech-to-text engine.
- **Usage Data and Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking services within the App. We do not collect or monitor your usage behavior.
- **Personal Identifiable Information (PII):** We do not require account registration, nor do we collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

## 3. Local Data Processing

All core functionalities of the App, including speech-to-text transcription using local models, are executed entirely on your Apple device. 
- **Processing:** The App utilizes your device's computational resources to process audio files and generate transcriptions. At no point does this data leave your device or get transmitted over the internet to our servers or any third-party servers.
- **Storage:** Any files, preferences, or settings generated or modified by the App are stored securely and locally on your device's file system or via Apple's secure environment.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Payments

While the App itself does not collect personal data, it relies on Apple Inc.'s infrastructure for distribution and financial transactions.

- **Apple StoreKit:** We utilize Apple's StoreKit framework to facilitate in-app purchases, including Monthly and Lifetime subscriptions. All payment processing, subscription management, and financial data collection are handled exclusively and securely by Apple. We do not have access to your credit card information, billing address, or any underlying financial details. 
For more information on how Apple handles your data, please review the Apple Privacy Policy at [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## 5. Data Security

Because Whisperer processes and stores all data locally, the security of your information is inherently tied to the security of your Apple device. We strongly recommend employing Apple's standard security features, such as strong passcodes, Touch ID, Face ID, and device encryption (FileVault or iOS Data Protection), to prevent unauthorized physical or logical access to your device and the data contained therein.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Given the offline nature of the App, we do not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we may take necessary actions.

## 7. User Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Other Jurisdictions)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data (e.g., the right to access, rectify, or erase your data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)). Because we do not collect, hold, or process any personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to access, rectify, or erase on your behalf. All data management remains entirely within your control on your local device.
